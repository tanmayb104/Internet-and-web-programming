Harry Potter and the philosopher's stone
by J. K. Rowling.
This book is about a boy who starts his journey in a magical school where he makes lots of friends and is faced with many challenges which he and his friends together overcome.
Harry Potter and the philosopher's stone is a great book. It is interesting and adventurous.
